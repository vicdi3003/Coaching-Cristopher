Hunter x Hunter

The story of Hunter x Hunter centers on a young man named Gon, who discovers that his father, who was told he was dead, is actually alive. Gon's father is a legendary hunter named Ging, a hunter whose recognition and worth is known to everyone.

HXH , Gon skills

Gon Hunter's abilities Gon Hunter has quite a few powers that make him a special being and will help him in his mission to become a hunter and thus be able to find his father.

  • He has enhanced or inhuman senses, such as a dog-like sense of smell and falcon-like vision, as well as a powerful sense of taste.
  • Gon is very skilled in hand-to-hand combat, this is due to having received training based on Shingen-Ryu Kung-fu by the master Biscuit Krueger.
  • When Gon has a fit of rage his powers are greatly increased, showing a series of seemingly unlimited talents.
  • Improved strength, Gon has great strength being able to break even a rock.
  • Accelerated healing, Gon displays an unusual ability to recover from very serious injuries.
  • Improved Speed and Reflexes, Gon has shown to have great speed, as well as great reflexes, reacting quickly to any danger. With his speed and agility he can create several afterimages of himself and disappear from sight.
  • Improved agility, he is very agile, being able to attack from any position.
