HXH GON

Hunter x Hunter sassortment of characters is undoubtedly one of the series strongest points. Most fans appreciate the characters and their wild dynamics more than any other aspect concerning the series. The characters are, after all, the common denominator between all the essential attributes that build up the Hunter x Hunter that fans love and care for.

Gon Freecs, the series is lead character, is one of the most controversial lead characters in shonen history. Love him or hate him, there is just too many things going on around Gon. He is innocent yet apathetic, happy-go-lucky yet very vengeful. His complexity is head and shoulders above most shonen leads. What is even more striking is how he disguises his complexity for simplicity.
